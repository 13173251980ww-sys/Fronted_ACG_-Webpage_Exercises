Founded in 2015, WhisperGames is a global video game publisher based in Xiamen, China. We provide multi-language text and audio localization services (including in-house voice over recording), PR, marketing, community support, maintenance, legal services, and more. We have developed strong partnerships in over 20 countries, and have provided customized publishing and localization solutions for over 100 video games. We are confident that we can help you reach out to worldwide audience.

About Us

  • Media Promotion and Exposure
    Visibility is critical and we can help by exposing your titles to more people in a crowded and ever-growing digital market. By working with the most popular streamers, game media, and social networks, we can help your games reach the right audience.
  • Community building and maintenance
    We provide high-quality & efficient customer services and maintain long-term relationships between you and Chinese players. Through our cooperation with largest Chinese Steam user communities and social media platforms, we can continuously inject innovation & vigor into the Chinese community.
  • Flexible Business Model
    We know how difficult it is to self-fund your next indie title, and we have some incredibly flexible payment solutions to help you with that. We often require no upfront payment and can potentially provide many of our services at no cost.
  • Localization and Support
    We are experienced with game localization, our sophisticated project process makes good use of CAT tools, glossaries, and translation memories to provide the best translation possible. We have a well-tested quality monitoring system and can provide LQA services for your game builds.
